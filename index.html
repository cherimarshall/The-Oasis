Practicing mindfulness can help one become more centered.
Listen to the calming music and reflect within.

1. Sit or lie comfortably
‍
2. Put one hand on your belly just below
your ribs and the other hand on your chest.
‍
3. Take a deep breath in through your nose, and
let your belly push your hand out.
‍
4. Breathe out through pursed lips. Feel the hand
on your belly go in, and use it to push all the air out.
‍
5. Do this breathing 3 to 10 times.
‍
6. Notice how you feel at the end of the exercise.
